Constituent Assembly (CA)

The Agenda “Debating the accession of Kashmir and the proposed special provisions for the state administration” centers upon the historical and current geopolitical conflict over Kashmir’s Status. This debate aims to examine the legal, political and social implications of Kashmir’s accession to India in the backdrop of the 1947 Indo-Pakistan war, along with the special provisions implemented to govern the region. The debate also considers the impact of these provisions on the state’s administration, autonomy and the effects of these provisions on the larger debate on federalism in India. We hope to have a vibrant discussion upon this issue, as relevant today at it was in 1949, and with the benefit of retrospect, perhaps reimagine history for India.

LOK SABHA

The agenda "Deliberating Reforms in the Special Marriage Act (1954), with Special Emphasis on Rights of LGBTQ+ Individuals" addresses the need to modernize India's legal framework to ensure it reflects the evolving understanding of human rights and equality. The Special Marriage Act was introduced in 1954 as a progressive step to establish a legal framework for marriages, offering an alternative framework outside the purview of religious laws. Despite being one of the most progressive legal provisions in modern India, the act still does not adequately protect or recognise the rights of LGBTQ+ individuals, leaving many without legal protections and privileges. This discussion puts the focus on the necessity of reforming the SMA to bring it up to par with similar legislatures in Asia and the rest of the world. We also hope to highlight the importance of including the recognition of same-sex marriages, protection against discrimination, and the legal rights of LGBTQ+ couples, such as adoption, inheritance, and spousal benefits. This debate also underscores the larger struggle for equality in India for cultural and religious minorities, seeking to build a society where all individuals, regardless of identity, are treated with equality and dignity.

International Police Organisation

The agenda "Deliberation on Prevention of Cybercrime and Ethics of Hacktivism with Special Emphasis on the Role of Artificial Intelligence" addresses critical issues pertinent to the modern digital era. The rise of cybercrime presents a major global challenge, as malicious actors exploit technology for fraudulent activities, data breaches, and other forms of digital misconduct. Alongside these concerns, the ethical implications of hacktivism—where hacking is employed as a tool for protest—are also under examination. Hacktivism, while drawing attention to significant causes, raises important questions regarding its legality, ethical justification, and potential unintended consequences. Artificial Intelligence (AI) further complicates this landscape. AI technology holds the promise of enhancing cybercrime prevention through sophisticated threat detection and response systems. Conversely, AI can be misused by cybercriminals to execute more advanced and destructive attacks, thereby presenting new challenges to conventional cybersecurity practices. The ethical deployment of AI in both combating and potentially exacerbating hacktivism remains a critical concern. This deliberation aims to explore strategies for effective cybercrime prevention, establish ethical standards for hacktivism, and determine the responsible use of AI in the digital realm. The objective is to achieve a balanced approach that upholds security, respects freedom of expression, and adheres to ethical principles in an increasingly interconnected global environment.

International Press Corp

The Press plays what is arguably the most crucial role in shaping and informing global affairs, it serves as both a watchdog and a bridge between governments and the global public. It informs the public opinion and serves as a force which holds powerful entities accountable exposing injustices, human rights abuses, and corruption on the international stage. By providing accurate and timely information, the media ensures transparency in global governance, enabling citizens to make informed decisions and participate in democratic processes. In today's time, the press is also under great danger, all around the world governments are trying hard to suppress independent voices which are critical of them. As we simulate the international press we hold onto hope that we will be able to raise awareness about the dangers the free press is in and motivate our participants to make their voices heard, unfettered.

United Nations Security Council

Political-Military-Industrial Nexus in international conflicts with special emphasis on ongoing crises in Ukraine and Palestine The agenda "Navigating Effects of the Political-Military-Industrial Nexus in International Conflicts" addresses the complex interplay between political, military, and industrial interests and their impact on global conflicts. This nexus profoundly influences the escalation, duration, and resolution of international crises. The ongoing crises in Ukraine and Palestine exemplify the effects of this nexus. In Ukraine, geopolitical strategies, military engagements, and defense industry interests have compounded the conflict, affecting both its intensity and trajectory. Similarly, in Palestine, the integration of political agendas, military actions, and industrial investments has perpetuated the crisis, shaping its management and resolution.This debate aims to examine how these interactions impact conflict dynamics, explore their implications for international stability, and discuss strategies for mitigating their detrimental effects on global peace efforts.

UNGA-DISEC

The agenda "Deliberating Upon Transparency in Biological Weapons Research and Proliferation of Biological Weapons with Special Emphasis on Development of Multilateral Contingency Strategies" focuses on the critical need for enhanced transparency and collaborative measures in addressing the threats posed by biological weapons. Biological weapons, due to their potential for mass harm, represent a significant global security challenge. The proliferation of these weapons necessitates stringent measures to ensure transparency in research and development activities. Effective monitoring and reporting mechanisms are essential to prevent the misuse of biological agents and to uphold international norms against their proliferation. The agenda also emphasizes the development of multilateral contingency strategies. These strategies are crucial for coordinated responses to biological threats, involving the collaboration of multiple nations to enhance preparedness, share critical information, and implement joint preventive and reactive measures.
